What is Freziva?

Freziva revolutionizes Nagapattinam’s seafood cold supply chain by leveraging advanced blockchain and IoT to ensure complete traceability and preliminary quality checks. Each catch is digitally verified, stored in isochoric cold storage, and linked to a unique QR code for transparency. The platform directly connects local fishermen with premium market buyers—upscale restaurants, hotels, and international catering events— bypassing intermediaries. This innovative solution organizes the unorganized sector, reduces waste, and strengthens rural supply chains while creating robust market linkages. By improving logistics and access to high-value markets, Fresiva enhances fishermen’s livelihoods and empowers local communities, ensuring sustainable profitability and growth. This drives regional prosperity.
